Documentation and Revitalization Projects

Ticha Project

Since 2013, I have been a research assistant on the Ticha Project, a digital text explorer of Colonial Zapotec documents.

Valley Zapotec Talking Dictionaries

In 2015-2016, as part of the Linguistics Field School (NSF REU Site Grant #1461056) in Oaxaca, I helped build Talking Dictionaries for three Valley Zapotec languages.

Indigenous Languge Engagement on Twitter

I actively engage with indigenous languages on Twitter (@mayhplumb). In 2016 and 2017 I participated in the Voces del Valle Project to encourage indigenous language speakers to write on Twitter (see #UsaTuVoz).
